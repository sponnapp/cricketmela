## 🔧 Setup Requirements

### Minimum Requirements:
- ✅ Laptop with Cricket Mela running
- ✅ Internet connection
- ✅ 4 terminal windows
- ✅ Friends' email addresses (optional)

- SSL certificate (free with Cloudflare/VPS)

4. **Test yourself first** - Before sending to friends
